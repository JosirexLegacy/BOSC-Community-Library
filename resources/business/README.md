\# Business Resources



This folder covers practical business and economics topics relevant to students

in commerce, management, and entrepreneurship programmes as well as anyone

interested in understanding how organisations and markets work.



\---



\## What's in This Folder



| File | Topic | Level |

|------|-------|-------|

| \[entrepreneurship-basics.md](entrepreneurship-basics.md) | Problem-solution fit, business models, bootstrapping | Beginner |

| \[financial-literacy.md](financial-literacy.md) | Budgeting, income vs profit, basic statements | Beginner |



\---



\## Recommended Starting Order



1\. \*\*financial-literacy.md\*\* — understanding money is foundational to everything else.

2\. \*\*entrepreneurship-basics.md\*\* — apply financial thinking to building something.
